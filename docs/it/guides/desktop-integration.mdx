---
title: "Integratio desktopi"
description: "Extrahe et installa automatice fasciculos .desktop et icones ex AppImages."
---

Integratio desktopi permittit AppImages tuos apparere automatice in initiantis applicationum desktopi tui. Cum habilitata, fp-appimage-updater extrahit manifestum `.desktop` et icones directe ex AppImage utendo `--appimage-extract`, et deinde ea installat in directoriis usori rectis.

## Quid installatur

| Typus fasciculi | Destinatio |
|---|---|
| Fasciculus `.desktop` | `~/.local/share/applications/` |
| Icones applicationis | `~/.local/share/icons/` |

Viae `Exec` et `Icon` intra fasciculum `.desktop` automatice renovantur ad indicandum locum installatum AppImage, ita initiator tuus semper utitur via binarii recta.

<Note>
  Integratio desktopi requirit ut AppImage `--appimage-extract` sustineat. Prope omnes AppImages moderni hoc sustinent.
</Note>

## Habilita vel dishabilita globaliter

Integratio desktopi per defaltam habilitata est. Pone `manage_desktop_files` in `config.yml` tuo ad eam globaliter moderandam:

```yaml
manage_desktop_files: true
```

Ad dishabilitandum integrationem pro omnibus applicationibus:

```yaml
manage_desktop_files: false
```

## Dishabilita per applicationem

Ad excludendum unicam applicationem ab integratione desktopi, pone `integration: false` in formula eius:

```yaml
name: myapp
integration: false
strategy:
  strategy: direct
  url: "https://example.com/myapp.AppImage"
  check_method: etag
```

<Tip>
  Si applicatio non apparet statim in initiantis tuo post renovationem, exsequere `update-desktop-database ~/.local/share/applications` ad renovandum antepositum menu applicationum.
</Tip>
